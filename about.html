FF ARENA

Built by Gamers, For Gamers.

FF Arena was born out of a simple frustration: participating in local Free Fire MAX tournaments was too chaotic. WhatsApp groups, manual payment screenshots, delayed prize money, and unfair matchmaking ruined the competitive experience.

We built this platform to bring professional esports infrastructure to the grassroots level in India. Instant wallets, automated leaderboards, and a verified organizer system ensure you spend less time waiting and more time securing Booyahs.

Rakshan Sharma

Founder & Lead Developer

Based in Aligarh, UP, Rakshan created FF Arena to elevate the Indian mobile esports scene. What started as a small project to host weekend scrims has evolved into the country's most advanced tournament ecosystem.
