Privacy Policy

Protecting your data in the Arena

1. Data Collection

To provide a seamless esports experience, we collect your in-game UID, registered email, and contact number. Payment transaction IDs and screenshots are securely stored via Supabase solely for verification purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to verify tournament entries, distribute prize pools to the correct accounts, and send critical match notifications (like Room IDs and Passwords).

3. Data Security

We employ enterprise-grade security protocols. We will never sell your personal information or Free Fire MAX UIDs to third-party advertisers.
