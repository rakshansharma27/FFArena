Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about competing on FF Arena.

Wallet & Payments

How do I deposit money into my Wallet?
Log into your User Dashboard, click on "Wallet", and select "Top Up". You can pay via any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm). The balance will reflect in your account immediately.
How long do prize payouts take?
Prize money is distributed directly to your Arena Wallet within 2 hours of match completion. You can withdraw your wallet balance to your bank via UPI at any time (processing takes 24 hours).

Tournaments & Matches

Where do I find the Custom Room ID and Password?
Room details will unlock in the "My Tournaments" section of your dashboard exactly 15 minutes before the scheduled match time.
Can I play with randoms?
You can register for Solo tournaments. For Squad or Duo tournaments, use our LFG (Looking For Group) board to find teammates and create a squad before registering.
