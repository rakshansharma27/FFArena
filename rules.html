FF ARENA

Official Rulebook

Standard competitive guidelines for all FF Arena tournaments.

1. Anti-Cheat & Fair Play

  • The use of emulators (Bluestacks, LDPlayer, etc.) is strictly prohibited unless the tournament is explicitly labeled as "PC Allowed".
  • Any team found using third-party modifications, aimbots, wallhacks, or scripts will face an immediate permanent ban and forfeiture of all wallet funds.
  • Teaming up with enemy squads in Solo/Duo/Squad modes is a bannable offense.
Admin Note: Organizers reserve the right to request screen recordings or handcam footage from any player at any time.

2. Standard Scoring System

  • 1 Kill = 1 Point
  • Booyah (1st) = 15 Points
  • 2nd Place = 12 Points
  • 3rd Place = 10 Points
  • 4th Place = 8 Points

3. Disconnections & Rematches

  • Matches will not be restarted for individual player disconnections or ping issues.
  • If a server crashes affecting more than 40% of the lobby, the match will be voided and restarted.
  • Players must join the custom room within the 15-minute grace period. The match will start exactly on time.
